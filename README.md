# Lifetime Vaccination Schedule
Federal judge Brian E. Murphy on March 16, 2026 issued a stay temporarily blocking portions of the HHS overhaul of childhood vaccination guidance proposed by HHS Secretary Robert F Kennedy Jr. The ruling effectively reverts the federal schedule to its pre-January 2026 state while the legal challenge proceeds.

As a thought experiment I asked three AI interfaces (Gemini, Grok, Claude)'s perspective  to think outside the box to come up with a vaccination schedule meeting the following parameters: maximize protection, minimize costs and the number of vaccines administered over one's lifetime using traditional, non-adjuvanted, non-mRNA/nanotech vaccines (to allay public's concerns about vaccines). I didn't indicate whether the vaccine schedule should be for the U.S. or the world and allowed the AI interface to select that parameter.

I've uploaded each AI interface's proposal. Gemini's proposal was unique in the sense that it was the only proposal to mention "Gatekeeper" responsibility and the use of Far-UVC lighting to kill germs.
